    About StandTall

    We use lightweight machine learning models to analyze posture in real time. This gives accurate, responsive feedback so you can reduce strain and improve posture over time.

    The Effects of Bad Posture

    Slouching and poor posture can lead to back and neck pain, reduced energy, and long-term health issues. Over time, it may affect your breathing, mood, and even your confidence.

    How our website helps: StandTall gives you instant feedback on your posture, helping you build healthy habits and reduce discomfort. With real-time tracking, you can improve your posture and feel better every day.
